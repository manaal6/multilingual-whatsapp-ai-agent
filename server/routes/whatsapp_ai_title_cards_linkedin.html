Why each line works on LinkedIn

01 "sells while you sleep" — triggers founder FOMO in the first 2 seconds. LinkedIn's algorithm rewards watch time; this forces a pause.
02 "AI understands. Instantly." — confident, no hedge words. LinkedIn audiences respond to certainty over explanation.
03 "It just works." — 3 words that eliminate objections before they form. Familiar enough to land, specific enough to mean something.
04 "It talks back" — the contrast with "type back" is the hook. Unexpected phrasing = re-watch.
05 "Auto-saved" — pain-point language. Every founder has lost a lead. This card hits that nerve directly.
06 "No spreadsheet needed" — everyone on LinkedIn has been burned by spreadsheet chaos. Instant relatability.
07 "Comment BUILD" — keyword comment CTAs boost LinkedIn reach algorithmically. "Your sales team got replaced" provokes a reaction share.
